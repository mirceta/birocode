Why not one background on the whole dock? Each region paints an opaque --color-surface, so a background on the outer .phone is fully covered. More coverage = tint each region individually. That's exactly what these three options trade off.

The point of the wash: when an agent is important, its border turns bright red — erasing the colour. The background tint is a separate property, so the colour survives. Flip + Important above to see border (red) and wash (colour) coexist.
